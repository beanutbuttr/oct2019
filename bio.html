Beatrice Hoang was born in Santa Clara, California and raised in Austin, Texas. She is currently a senior at the Louisiana School for Math, Science, and the Arts (LSMSA). She enjoys exploring interests in both visual and performing arts and in computer science, taking various classes in drawing and painting, photography, design, piano performance, C++ and JAVA.

As a pianist, she has won prizes across the US, resulting in performances with the Austin Civic Orchestra, Round Rock Symphony, and Monroe Symphony Orchestra. She debuted at Carnegie Hall in May 2019.

Beatrice is also a mathlete. She especially likes number sense and problem solving. She has won awards at the Texas Math and Science Coaches Association state high school competitions and Texas Association of Private and Parochial Schools state academic competitions. She is a National Merit Semifinalist.

Before she attended LSMSA, Beatrice attended St. Dominic Savio Catholic High School in Austin, Texas. She now lives in Cupertino, California, and in her free time she enjoys longboarding, solving Rubik’s cubes, embroidering, and swimming. She plans to attend Brown University where she will major in Computer Science and Modern Culture and Media.
